Everything Is Spatial (Week 1)

My Favorite Places

In New York

Nitehawk Cinema Is New York’s premier dine-in theater.

The most wonderful indie theater, where you can find all the weird, nostalgic films. The food is banging and I love how they have specials inspired by the movies they show.

High Line Is an elevated freight rail line transformed into a public park on Manhattan’s West Side.

The landscaping, design and concept of this park is incredible! It's a "recycled" train track turned park. Great place to catch a sunset of the city and take a nice walk with an amazing view.

Dia:Beacon Is a museum for the Dia Art Foundation's collection of art.

Great collection of minimalist art, many are site specific installations making Dia a very special place.
